BOCSH
====================

**what is bocsh ?**

Bocsh is a portable and lightweight userspace designed for minimal distros and/or custom platforms and embedded devices 


**why bocsh ?**

bocsh is made to be the perfect option for developers who want portability . minimalism . and functionality with speed and performance ontop over bloat and stuffed userspaces


**when was bocsh created and why ?**

it was originally established and created for the OhOs operating system by Michael Flick. even though it works fine with linux since Oh was linux compatible originally. it still remains deeply linked to its phillosophy of minimalism.
